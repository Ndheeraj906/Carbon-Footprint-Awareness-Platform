# Requirement Analysis & User Personas

## Problem Statement
Climate change requires individual action, but users lack visibility into their personal carbon footprint and actionable steps to reduce it.

## User Personas

### 1. "Eco-Curious Emma"
- **Age**: 28
- **Goal**: Wants to understand her impact but finds calculations overwhelming.
- **Needs**: Simple UI, clear metrics, and gamification to stay motivated.

### 2. "Sustainability Sam"
- **Age**: 35
- **Goal**: Actively wants to reduce emissions by 20% this year.
- **Needs**: Precise calculation logs, historical trends, and strict goal tracking.

## Core Workflows
1. **Onboarding**: Secure Google Auth login.
2. **Awareness**: Viewing the "Eco Score" dashboard.
3. **Action**: Logging daily transport or diet activities.
4. **Improvement**: Receiving AI-logic recommendations and achieving Badges.
